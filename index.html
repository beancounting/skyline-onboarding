Welcome to Skyline Accounting's Client Onboarding

As a regulated accounting practice under Australia's AML/CTF Act 2006, we are required to collect and verify certain information about every client before providing services. This form is confidential and used for compliance purposes only.

This form supports multiple entities. If you have companies, trusts, partnerships or other structures, you can add all of them in one session. People who appear in more than one entity (e.g. as both a director and a trustee) only need to be entered once.

Before you begin: Have the following ready — photo ID (driver's licence or passport), ABN/ACN for any businesses, and trust deed details if applicable. You can upload a copy of your photo ID directly in this form. You will also receive a separate Seamlss link to complete formal identity verification.
Using a shared or public computer? Do not use this form on a shared device. Your progress is saved in this browser. If you must stop, use the "Start over" option when you return.
Privacy notice: The information you provide is collected under the Anti-Money Laundering and Counter-Terrorism Financing Act 2006 (Cth). It will be stored securely, accessed only by authorised Skyline Accounting staff, and retained for 7 years as required by law. You have the right to request access to your information at any time.
