8 Politically Exposed Person (PEP) declaration
What is a PEP? A Politically Exposed Person is someone who holds (or has held within the last 2 years) a prominent public position — such as a senior government official, politician, judge, military officer, ambassador, or senior executive of a state-owned enterprise — in Australia or overseas. Close family members and known associates of such persons are also considered PEPs.
Our AML/CTF Compliance Officer will follow up with you to clarify this. Please continue with the form.
